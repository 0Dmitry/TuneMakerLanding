Tune Maker User Manual

    Pro Subscription

    Tune Maker is a free app with an optional Pro subscription. You can use the app without Pro subscription with some limitation in functionality (see Free vs Pro).

    I know subscriptions are annoying, but it is the only way I see that will allow me to continue working on Tune Maker full-time. Even when an app is already on the App Store you need to continue working on it to adopt for OS changes, fix bugs and add new features. I have big plans for this app so my work on it just started. The more users will agree to get the Pro subscription the more chances to make this app really great.

    Hope for your understanding,
    Dmitry
