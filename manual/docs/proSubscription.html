    Pro Subscription

    Tune Maker is a free app with an optional Pro Subscription. You can use the app without Pro Subscription with some limitation in features (see Free vs Pro).

    When you buy Pro Subscription, you:

    • Get access to all the advanced features of Tune Maker.
    • Support the Tune Maker team and allows us to continue working on Tune Maker, adding new awesome features and improving overall quality of the app.
