    Tune View

    Overview

    Tune View is the main view that shows you the list of the tune's sections. Each section shows 4 rows, one row for each component: Melody, Arpeggio, Drums and Chords.

    Section operations

    Open Section

    To open a section, double-tap on it.

    Create new Section

    To insert a new empty section after the currently selected one, tap the plus button at the bottom right.

    Delete Section

    To delete a section slide it to the left and tap Delete.

    Clone or Repeat Section

    You can create a new section by duplicating an existing one. Slide a section to the right and tap Clone. If you want to repeat a section, tap Repeat.

    When you clone a section you create a new independent instance. When you repeat a section you create a reference to the existing section. So if you edit the repeated section, both the original section and all the repeats will be affected.

    Reorder section

    To move a section to the new position, long-press on it and then start dragging it vertically.

    Transport control buttons

    To start the playback hit play. To pause playback hit play again or hit stop. To return playhead position to the beginning of the current section hit stop again. To return playhead to the beginning of the tune hit stop once more.

    Play modes

    There are three play modes available:

    • Loop whole tune. The whole tune is played repeatedly.
    • Loop one section. The selected section is played repeatedly.
    • Play to the end. Plays from the selected section until the tune's end and stops.

    Tempo & Metronome

    Tap the metronome icon to open the tempo & metronome dialog.

    Metronome Modes

    • Off - Metronome is disabled.
    • Pre-Roll - Metronome only plays during the recording pre-roll.
    • Recording - Metronome plays during recording.
    • Always - Metronome plays always
